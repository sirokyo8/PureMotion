Chest Workout

A chest workout primarily targets the pectoral muscles, which are located in the front of the chest. It's important to include a variety of exercises to work different parts of the chest.

  • Bench Press
  • Push-Ups
  • Chest Flys
  • Cable Crossovers
×

Back Workout

A back workout aims to strengthen the muscles of the back, including the latissimus dorsi, rhomboids, and traps. Strong back muscles are essential for good posture and overall strength.

  • Pull-Ups
  • Rows (Barbell, Dumbbell, Cable)
  • Deadlifts
  • Lat Pulldowns
×

Bicep Workout

A bicep workout focuses on building and defining the bicep muscles, which are located on the front of the upper arm. Strong biceps are essential for lifting and pulling movements.

  • Bicep Curls (Barbell, Dumbbell, Cable)
  • Hammer Curls
  • Concentration Curls
  • Preacher Curls
×

Tricep Workout

A tricep workout targets the tricep muscles, which are located on the back of the upper arm. Strong triceps are important for pushing movements and overall arm strength.

  • Tricep Dips
  • Tricep Extensions (Overhead, Rope)
  • Skull Crushers
  • Tricep Kickbacks
×

Shoulder Workout

A shoulder workout focuses on strengthening the deltoid muscles, which are responsible for shoulder movement. Strong shoulders are important for overall upper body strength and stability.

  • Shoulder Press (Barbell, Dumbbell)
  • Lateral Raises
  • Front Raises
  • Upright Rows
×

Leg Workout

A leg workout targets the muscles of the lower body, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. Strong legs are essential for stability, balance, and overall functional movement.

  • Squats (Back Squats, Front Squats)
  • Lunges (Forward, Reverse, Walking)
  • Leg Press
  • Calf Raises
